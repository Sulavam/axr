We work hard to
achieve our goals

DBM Foundation strives for a world without unnecessary blindness and vision impairment. Making proper treatment for cataracts and having eyeglasses and contact lenses possible for everyone who needs them.

Our dream

Our dream is to end avoidable blindness and vision impairment and improve the socioeconomic and sociocultural position of the disenfranchised.

Our mission

Our mission is to eliminate avoidable blindness and vision impairment to support people and enable them to contribute to the socioeconomic growth of their society.

Our vision

We imagine a world where people with good vision can reach their full potential.

How we make this happen in the long term:

We put our plans into three concrete goals:

  • Avoidable blindness eliminated
  • Effective and accessible cataract treatment
  • Effective and accessible prevention and treatment of vision impairments

Donations make our work possible

Our activities for good eye care are possible through active funding. DBM Foundation runs entirely on donations, large and small. Donations make our work possible; without them, these regions have no opportunities for good eye care. We use our donations directly for all our activities.

Our activities are completely clear

Our focus is on improving, preventing and curing. This is what we do:

  • Training local medical personnel and volunteers
  • Setting up field offices for general support and medical management
  • Building eye clinics at easy-to-reach locations
  • Donating medical eye care equipment
  • Financing cataract surgeries
  • Eye exams for elderly and schoolchildren
  • Prescribing eyeglasses

Together for better vision

Working towards ensuring quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods for underserved communities across India.
