Promoting Wellness

Public Health Programme

We promote preventive health awareness, screenings, education, and early intervention empowering communities with reliable information and practices that support long term well-being.

These efforts aim to strengthen community understanding of health and promote practices that support healthier living through behavior change and informed decision making.

Our Core Domains

Preventive Health Awareness

Designing campaigns on behaviour change practices around hygiene, nutrition, and vaccination to reduce risks.

Community Health Education

Workshops and village meetings to build health literacy and encourage informed decision-making.

Early Detection & Screening

Regular health camps, eye screenings, and early detection of disabilities or conditions like anemia.

Advancing Community Wellness

Empowering communities through preventive health awareness, early intervention, and screenings that support long-term well being.

Glimpses From The Field

Working towards ensuring quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods for underserved communities across India.
