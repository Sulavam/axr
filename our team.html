Our organization

From our Amsterdam headquarters Eye Care Foundation supports our international teams. We currently work in Vietnam, Nepal, Cambodia, Laos, Surinam, Ecuador and Tanzania. Further expansion of our work areas and collaborations with other organisations is part of our roadmap.

Our Team

Meet the leaders driving our mission forward.

Mr. Samar Mahapatro – Social Entrepreneur & Leader

Samar Mahapatro is a dynamic social entrepreneur and business leader committed to scalable social impact and sustainable enterprise. With over 15 years of experience spanning finance, operations, and social innovation, Samar has been instrumental in driving results-oriented change across healthcare, technology, and community development sectors.

Mr. Amarjit Jena – Team Member

A dedicated professional committed to the foundation's goals, bringing expertise and passion to support our community initiatives and operational success.

Mr. Akhilesh Parida – Entrepreneur & Social Impact Leader

Akhilesh Parida is an entrepreneur and social impact leader with over two decades of experience across engineering, hospitality, live events, energy logistics, and infrastructure services. He is the CEO & Managing Partner of Utkal Hospitality Services, Founder of InterXcel LLP, and Additional Director at Doctors Beyond Medicine Foundation.

Ms. Anita – Social Development Professional

Anita is a Social Development Professional experienced in Government and Corporate Partnerships, with a strong foundation in social work (DSSW) and experience as a Public Health Trainer. She works at the intersection of program execution, stakeholder coordination, and community impact. She is also an aspirant Clinical Psychologist.

Working towards ensuring quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods for underserved communities across India.
